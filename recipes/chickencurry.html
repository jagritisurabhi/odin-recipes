Chicken Curry

Above image is just for reference but in no way not how juicy this will turn out to be!

The ingredients for the recipe can be found under here:

  • Chicken curry cut 500g
  • Chopped onions (medium) 2 pieces
  • Slit green chillies (long) 2 pieces
  • Chopped tomatoes (medium) 2 pieces
  • Ginger Garlic paste 1tbsp
  • Whisked curd 3 spoons
  • Red chilly powder 2tbsp
  • Turmeric powder 1tbsp
  • Coriander powder 2 tbsp
  • Garam Masala powder 2.5tbsp
  • Salt 3 tbsp
  • Raw seasonings - Cumin seeds (1tbsp), Caradamom(2), Dry paprika(1), Starlise(1), Cloves(2), Black Pepper(3), Cinnamon(2cm stick), Big Elaichi(1), Bay Leaf(1)
  • Ghee/Oil (2tbsp)
  • Coriander leaves (for garnish)

Cooking steps

  1. Take washed curry cut chicken in a bowl and drain out all the water off it. Wash it in warm water with salt mixed for better results
  2. Heat Oil/Ghee in a Colander
  3. Put all the raw seasonings in it till it starts sizzling
  4. Put slit green chillies followed by onions after the chillies start to sizzle
  5. When the onions turn transclucent brown, put the ginger garlic paste and let it heat for a minute till the raw smell of both goes away
  6. Put the cut tomatoes and let it heat up till all the water is gone and it becomes soggy
  7. Put the powders - Red chilly, turmeric, salt, garam masala and coriander in the specified quantities
  8. Put whisked curd and let the thick paste dissolve into it
  9. Put the chicken pieces in the colander along with 2 cups of water and let it sit on stove for 15-20 minutes on medium heat
  10. Turn off the flame and granish with coriander leaves
  11. Serve hot with steamed rice or Indian flat breads
